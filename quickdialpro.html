Depiction

QuickDialPro

Description

Compatible with iOS 10

QuickDial Pro implements 3D touch quick actions to the dial buttons in the phone app compared to QuickDial Lite which allows a long press to simply call a number, Pro allows you to 3D Touch or long press (if you device does not have 3D Touch) to display a menu displaying buttons to call, message or facetime a specific number. In the settings you can set the number for each dial button. Featuring 3D touch quick action menus for all the dial numbers in the native phone app. If you device does not support 3D touch then you can use a long press to display the menu.
