InfiniOS

Information:

Compatible with iOS 8 & 9

InfiniOS is the tweak of all tweaks, allowing it's user to customise every available feauture of the iOS system from 'Stock iOS Apps' to 'System Functions'.

Have you ever wanted to change the way your Control Center looks? Well now you can by simply opening the options found inside of settings and edit away. How about editing the Messages bubble colour? You can do that too with Chatty found in the InfiniOS settings.

With a huge range of customisation available already and tons more to come. You will truly never run out of variations.
