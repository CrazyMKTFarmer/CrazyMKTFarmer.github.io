RotoLock

RotoLock is a small tweak requested by Reddit user /u/xfzd25 which forces an iPad style landscape mode on the lockscreen for all devices.

Compatiblility:
- iPhone 4S, 5, 5C, 5S, 6, 6+, 6S, 6S+
- iPad's not tested yet
- iPod Touch 5, 6
iOS Version:
- iOS 9.0.2 - 9.3.3
Bugs:
- Doesn't rotate as it should on all devices

CamHDR+

CamHDR+ is a small tweak requested by Reddit user /u/mingming91 which permanently enables the HDR Mode of the camera app.

Compatiblility:
- iPhone 4S, 5, 5C, 5S, 6, 6+, 6S, 6S+
- iPad's not tested yet
- iPod's not tested yet
iOS Version:
- iOS 9
Bugs:
- None

InfiniOS

InfiniOS is a tweak similar to Springtomize , but with the option to edit tons more items in the future, InfiniOS will eventually let you customise every stock app of iOS and all system sections ie; Lockscreen, Homescreen, Control Center, Notification Center, Status Bar and more.

Compatiblility:
- iPhone 4S, 5, 5C, 5S, 6, 6+, 6S, 6S+
- iPad's not tested yet
- iPod Touch 5, 6
iOS Version:
- iOS 8 & 9
Bugs:
- Crashes when libcolorpicker isn't installed
