NoSleepPls

NoSleepPls is a simple tweak. Requested by u/sjjose2001. This tweak let's you easily change the fade timer to any amount(in seconds).

Compatiblility:
- iPhone 4S, 5, 5C, 5S, 6, 6+, 6S, 6S+
- iPad's not tested yet
- iPod Touch 5, 6
iOS Version:
- iOS 9
Bugs:
- Initial installation timer is set to 0, I recommend to respring and hold the volume '+' button, then you should be able to unlock to change the timer
